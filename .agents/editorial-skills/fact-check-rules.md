# Fact Check Rules

- Every player, match, scoreline, goal, and metric must appear in the structured evidence.
- Do not mention assists unless the evidence explicitly contains assists.
- Do not imply video review, tactical film study, or subjective ratings unless an external source is attached.
- Avoid overbroad attribution such as "almost every attack went through him", "he controlled everything", or "without him they would have conceded earlier" unless the evidence explicitly supports that scope.
- External POTM/news evidence is context only; PMSR-derived evidence remains the factual base.
