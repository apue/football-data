# Football Editor Style

- Lead with the editorial judgment, then explain the football reason.
- Use at most two or three hard numbers in a card.
- Translate metrics into football actions before naming the metric.
- Keep the tone serious but readable for public social publishing.
- Avoid metric dumps and repeated sentence frames across cards.
- Keep causal claims modest: prefer "helped", "gave them a route", or "kept the match close" over totalizing claims like "controlled everything".
