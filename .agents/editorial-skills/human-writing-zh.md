# Human Chinese Sports Writing

- Start from what happened in the match: goal, pressure, movement, receiving, defending, or progression.
- Use short paragraphs with a clear first sentence.
- If the evidence is obvious, say it plainly.
- If the evidence is subtle, explain why the repeated action mattered.
- Keep names and numbers readable; convert long metre values to kilometres when helpful.
- Use the standard football spelling “首开纪录”, not “首开记录”.
- Do not write “机会全部打进” or “射门全部打进” unless the evidence explicitly shows shots equal goals.
- Do not invent details such as “禁区抢点”, “防线身前接应”, “不敢前压”, “从压力里带出来”, “最稳定的推进出口”, or “组织反扑” unless those exact facts appear in evidence.
